Examples of Agent Based Models

Forest Fires (NetLogo)


An agent based model included in the NetLogo install. This models the movement of a fire through a forest, with a basic visualization.
I have a tutorial covering installing NetLogo, loading the existing model and making some modifications.

Boids (Unity)


You might have wondered why most of the images used in this article have been of birds (or at least my best attempt at representing birds). This is because of one of a classic agent based model designed in 1986 by Craig Reynolds.
The boids are agents designed to simulate flocking behaviour, similar to that exhibited by certain species of birds and fish.
I am working on a full tutorial demonstrating boids in a three dimensional space using Unity as the platform.

Tax vs. Trade (Java)


This was a research project I completed for my bachelors thesis.
Here I constructed an agent based model in Java, using power companies as agents trading in electricity production facilities. Different economic restrictions were applied to the agents, with varying levels of Carbon Tax or Carbon Credit caps being used.
The thesis may be found in the University of Groningen archives here.
The model and its code, along with JavaDoc may be found here this repository also contains python notebooks for easily running the model and visualizing its results.
