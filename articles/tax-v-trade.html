Tax vs. Trade

An agent-based model to compare the efficacy of different emission reduction measures.
This page is an overview of the project, containing the motivation, methods used and summary of the results. The full paper may be read here. The code may be found on GitHub here.

Carbon Tax

  • Sets a price per unit of CO2 produced
  • Incurred cost scales with the amount of CO2
  • No upper limit on emissions

Cap and Trade

  • Allocates credits allowing for CO2 production
  • Provides an upper limit on emissions
  • Credits may be bought or sold

Agent-Based Model

A simulation of multiple agents interacting with one another in order to model possible outcomes.
